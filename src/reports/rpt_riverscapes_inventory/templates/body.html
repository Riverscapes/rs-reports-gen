Appendix 2 - Source Data

This report was generated using data from the Riverscapes Data Exchange. Free, pubicly accessibable data were processed through a series of models by the Riverscapes Consortium, culminating with the Riverscapes Metric Engine (RME) that synthesizes metrics together for all the preceeding models. These RME data are based on polygons that span the valley bottom and are segmented at a distance that varies with drainage area. These polygons, or discreet geographic objects (DGOs) possess over 100 metrics spanning a variety of topics pertaining to riverscape health.

The model runs in question were all part of an initiative to produce a continuous riverscapes dataset for the Conterminous United States in 2025.
