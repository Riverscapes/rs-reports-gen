Appendix 2 - Source Data

This report was generated using data from the Riverscapes Data Exchange. Free, pubicly accessibable data were processed through a series of models by the Riverscapes Consortium, to produce a Riverscapes Dynamics output. The segmentation of the river into polygons, or Discrete Geographic Objects (DGOs) allows many metrics relating to river health to be applied to a suitably sized stretch of riverscape area.

The model runs in question were all part of an initiative for New Zealand Riverscapes by North Arrow Research.
