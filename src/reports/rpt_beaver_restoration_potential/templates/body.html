The Beaver Restoration Potential report combines information regarding the capacity of riverscapes to support beaver dam building as modeled by BRAT with information on existing beaver dam building activity. If data on beaver dam locations has been collected and made available, this report can be used to estimate the realized beaver dam building capacity within the report area. This information can be used to identify areas where beaver dam building activity is occurring and where it is not occurring, which can inform restoration efforts and management decisions.

Realized Beaver Dam Building Capacity

The following metrics summarize the state of beaver dam building capacity within the report area. The Total Dam Capacity is the number of dams that the perennial riverscapes in the report area could support based on BRAT capacity estimates. The Total Dams is the number of dams present in the report area according to imagery-based dam identification (see associated projects, Appendix _). The Realized Capacity is the percentage of the total dam building capacity that currently exists in the report area based on this data.

Realized Dam Building Capacity in Highest Restoration Potential Riverscapes

The following metrics summarize the state of beaver dam building capacity within the riverscapes with the highest restoration potential according to BRAT. These are streams where there is high capacity to support dam building AND low risk to infrastructure. These riverscapes correspond to the 'Conservation/Approporiate for Translocation' and 'Encourage Beaver Expansion/Colonization' output categories in the BRAT conservation/restoration opportunities output. The Total Dam Capacity is the number of dams that the perennial riverscapes with the highest restoration potential in the report area could support based on BRAT capacity estimates. The Total Dams is the number of dams present in the riverscapes with the highest restoration potential according to imagery-based dam identification (see associated projects, Appendix _). The Realized Capacity is the percentage of the total dam building capacity that currently exists in the riverscapes with the highest restoration potential in the report area based on this data.
