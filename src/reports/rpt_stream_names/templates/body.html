The riverscapes stream names report provides insight into the current state and health of riverine ecosystems. It pulls together data from a variety of public sources and combines them with the results from several models covering a range of topics from valley bottom identification, beaver restoration and riparian condition. The goal is to provide a broad overview of the riverscape and the landscape context in which it resides.
